Data Cleaning & Preparation

Using statistical techniques, I uncover trends, patterns, and insights hidden within data. From hypothesis testing to predictive modeling, I provide actionable insights that help organizations make data-driven decisions.

SQL is essential to my workflow, enabling me to query, manage, and manipulate large datasets with precision. Excel

Excel is a powerful tool in my data analysis toolkit, perfect for quick data exploration, reporting, and visualization.
